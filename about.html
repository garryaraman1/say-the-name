SEVENTEEN IS 13! ✨

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group under PLEDIS Entertainment. Debuting in 2015, they are known as a self-producing idol group, actively participating in songwriting, choreography, and production.

The group consists of 13 members divided into 3 units: Hip-Hop Team, Vocal Team, and Performance Team.
